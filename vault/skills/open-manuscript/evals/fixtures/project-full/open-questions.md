# Open Questions — Collab-IEM-NanoplasticZeolites

## [OPEN] Is TraPPE-UA transferable to oxidised PP fragments? (added 2026-02-28)

TraPPE-UA was parameterised for alkanes. PP fragments in weathered nanoplastic may carry carbonyl/hydroxyl groups. No literature precedent for TraPPE-UA on oxidised polyolefin in zeolite pores.

## [OPEN] What is the effect of Al substitution on adsorption selectivity? (added 2026-03-15)

All DFT and GCMC so far use all-silica MFI. Real zeolites have Al sites that change electrostatics. Reviewer will likely ask about this — do we need at least one Al-site calculation?

## [CLOSED] Should we use BSSE correction for DFT energies? (resolved 2026-02-15) → Yes — counterpoise correction applied to all final Table 5 values; BSSE is 7–10 kJ/mol (significant).
