## Relevance rubric

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|---------|
| **High** | Directly covers an active research area or project; you would submit an abstract or cite heavily |
| **Medium** | Adjacent field; useful for background, collaboration, or broadening; worth attending if nearby |
| **Low** | Outside main focus; only marginally relevant; note but do not prioritise |
| **Skip** | Completely outside your field; no action needed |
