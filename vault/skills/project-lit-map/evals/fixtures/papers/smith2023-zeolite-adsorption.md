# Smith 2023 — DFT Study of Polymer Adsorption in Zeolite Pores

**Authors:** A. Smith, B. Jones  
**Venue:** Journal of Physical Chemistry C | **Year:** 2023 | **Citations:** 45  
**Why included:** Core method reference for DFT adsorption calculations

## Key points *(drafted — verify after reading)*
- **Methods:** DFT/PBE-D3 with plane-wave basis, 830 eV cutoff
- **Key result:** E_ads ranges from -0.2 to -1.4 eV depending on pore geometry
- **Limitations:** Only tested on pure-silica frameworks
- **Relevance to project:** Provides DFT methodology for adsorption energy calculation in BEA and MFI zeolites directly applicable to our CASTEP workflow.
