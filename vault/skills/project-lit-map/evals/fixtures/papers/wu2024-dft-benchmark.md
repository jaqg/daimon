# Wu 2024 — Benchmarking Dispersion Corrections for Zeolite Adsorption

**Authors:** I. Wu, J. Kim  
**Venue:** Journal of Chemical Theory and Computation | **Year:** 2024 | **Citations:** 12  
**Why included:** DFT functional selection for adsorption calculations

## Key points *(drafted — verify after reading)*
- **Methods:** Benchmarking PBE, PBE-D3, PBE-D3(BJ) against experimental heats of adsorption
- **Key result:** PBE-D3 within 5% of experiment for small molecules in BEA; PBE without dispersion underestimates by 30-50%
- **Limitations:** Only tested alkanes/alkenes, not polymers
- **Relevance to project:** Justifies our PBE-D3 choice over PBE-only for BEA calculations; directly supports our methodology section.
