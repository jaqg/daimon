# Liu 2021 — GCMC Simulation of Polymer Fragment Adsorption

**Authors:** E. Liu, F. Zhang  
**Venue:** Langmuir | **Year:** 2021 | **Citations:** 78  
**Why included:** Core GCMC methodology reference

## Key points *(drafted — verify after reading)*
- **Methods:** GCMC with COMPASS III force field, fugacity from Peng-Robinson EOS
- **Key result:** Henry coefficients for ethylene in ZSM-5 at 298 K
- **Limitations:** Single-component only, no competitive adsorption
- **Relevance to project:** GCMC methodology and COMPASS III force field we use for adsorption isotherms; validates our force-field choice against experimental data.
